# Banking Client Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Banking Client Analytics solution developed using MySQL, Python, and Power BI. The objective is to analyze banking customer data, identify key business trends, evaluate customer financial behavior, and provide actionable insights through interactive dashboards.

The project combines data extraction, cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and business intelligence reporting to support data-driven decision-making in the banking domain.

---

## Business Problem

Banks manage large volumes of customer data across multiple financial products and services. Understanding customer demographics, income levels, account balances, lending behavior, and financial engagement is essential for improving customer retention, risk assessment, and business growth.

This project aims to:

- Analyze customer financial profiles
- Identify high-value customer segments
- Evaluate banking product utilization
- Monitor key business metrics
- Support strategic decision-making through data visualization

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Collection
- Imported banking customer datasets.
- Validated data quality and structure.

### 2. Data Preparation
- Cleaned missing and inconsistent values.
- Created derived variables and customer segments.
- Categorized customers based on income levels.

### 3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Customer demographic analysis
- Income distribution analysis
- Product ownership analysis
- Banking account utilization analysis
- Correlation analysis among financial indicators

### 4. Dashboard Development
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize:

- Customer demographics
- Income segmentation
- Credit card usage
- Deposit and savings trends
- Loan distribution
- Risk classifications
- Key banking KPIs

---

## Key Insights

- Customers with higher bank deposits tend to maintain larger balances across checking, savings, and foreign currency accounts.
- Income segmentation helps identify high-value customer groups.
- Product ownership patterns reveal opportunities for cross-selling banking services.
- Customer financial behavior can support improved risk assessment and relationship management strategies.
